## What is it?

This offence covers penetration of the vagina or anus with a body part (other than a penis — that would be rape) or an object, without consent. This includes fingers, tongue, or any object. It is as serious as rape in law and carries the same maximum sentence.

## Is this a crime?

**Yes.** This is one of the most serious sexual offences and carries the same maximum sentence as rape.

## The law says...

Sexual Offences Act 2003, Section 2 — it is an offence for a person intentionally to penetrate the vagina or anus of another person with a part of their body or anything else, where the penetration is sexual, the complainant does not consent, and the defendant does not reasonably believe they consent.

## To prove this, police need to show:

1. The suspect intentionally penetrated the vagina or anus of another person with a part of their body or anything else
2. The penetration was sexual
3. The complainant did not consent
4. The suspect did not reasonably believe the complainant consented

## Signs this may be happening to you

- Someone has penetrated you with their fingers, tongue, or an object without your consent
- You felt unable to refuse because of fear, intoxication, or coercion
- You were asleep, unconscious, or otherwise unable to consent
- Someone used force or threats to carry out the act

## What to do right now

1. **Get somewhere safe** — your safety comes first
2. **If you can, preserve forensic evidence** — don't wash or change clothes if possible
3. **Contact a SARC** (Sexual Assault Referral Centre) — they provide medical care and forensic examination
4. **Call the Rape Crisis helpline** (0808 802 9999) — free, 24/7
5. **You can report to police when you are ready** — there is no time limit

## Maximum sentence

**Life imprisonment**

## Common myths — the truth

| Myth | Truth |
|------|-------|
| "It's only serious if it involves a penis" | Assault by penetration carries the same maximum sentence as rape — life imprisonment |
| "It was just fingers, it doesn't count" | Any non-consensual penetration is a serious criminal offence |
